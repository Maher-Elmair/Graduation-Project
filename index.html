Toshiba history

The words that launched Toshiba’s founding history.

Toshiba's roots go back to a small shop and factory in Ginza's "brick town," founded in 1875, and Hakunetsu-sha & Co., Ltd., founded in 1890. Hisashige Tanaka, nicknamed karakuri giemon; the genius of mechanical wonders, was one of Japan's greatest inventors. Ichisuke Fujioka, a wizard with electricity, was known as the Japanese Edison.
